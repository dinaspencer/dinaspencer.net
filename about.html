An American in Spain

Dina Spencer is a front-end developer diving deeper into JavaScript, React, Angular and the full tech stack. She's also a freelance communication coach and writer, using native English and advanced Spanish to refine professional communication. Originally from Winston-Salem, North Carolina, she has been living in Spain since 2013. She has lived in Bilbao, Castro-Urdiales, Ferrol, Salamanca, Santander, León, Madrid and Zaragoza.

Interests: photography, running, cycling, being outdoors, personal finance, traveling around Spain, and becoming an expert on Spanish pueblos.
